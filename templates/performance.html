Why ML — Not Deep Learning?

✅ ML Advantages for SMS

→Dataset (5,572 msgs) is too small for deep neural networks to generalise properly
→SMS text is short and structured — ideal for statistical ML models
→ML gives interpretable coefficients — the backbone of our XAI explanation
→Training time: seconds vs hours for transformer models
→Achieves 98%+ accuracy — no performance gap with DL for this task

⚠️ DL Limitations Here

→BERT/LSTM need 100,000+ samples; we have 5,572 — risk of overfitting
→Deep Learning is a black box — cannot explain why a message is spam
→Requires GPU infrastructure — not suitable for lightweight deployment
→For SMS classification, DL adds complexity without meaningful accuracy gain
→Our ML system already exceeds typical DL baselines on this dataset
